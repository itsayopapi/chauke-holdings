Company Overview

Delivering excellence through diversified operations and strategic partnerships.

Chauke Group Holdings is a dynamic, multidisciplinary company committed to delivering a broad spectrum of professional services across various sectors. Our business model is built on flexibility, innovation, and strategic partnerships, allowing us to adapt to diverse client needs and market demands.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and development

2009 - Foundation

Chauke Group Holdings was established with a vision to create a diversified holding company serving multiple industries.

2012 - Expansion

Expanded our service portfolio to include logistics and supply chain management, establishing key partnerships.
