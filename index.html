Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and development since 2016

2016 - Foundation

Chauke Group Holdings was established with a vision to create a diversified holding company serving multiple industries across South Africa.

2017 - Initial Growth

Secured our first major contracts in professional services and established key partnerships with government institutions.

2018 - Expansion

Expanded our service portfolio to include logistics, supply chain management, and infrastructure development services.

2019 - Diversification

Entered the energy solutions market and established our information technology division to offer comprehensive digital services.

2020 - Resilience

Adapted to global challenges by developing innovative remote service delivery models and expanding our digital capabilities.

2021 - Strategic Partnership

Acquired significant stake in Plethora Plastic Engineers, expanding our manufacturing capabilities and industrial footprint.

2022 - National Recognition

Gained recognition as a trusted partner for major national projects and expanded our team to over 50 dedicated professionals.

2023 - Innovation & Technology

Invested in cutting-edge technologies and sustainable solutions, positioning ourselves as leaders in green energy and digital transformation.

2024 - Vision 2030

Launched our strategic roadmap for sustainable growth, international expansion, and continued excellence in service delivery.

Strategic Partnership

Chauke Group Holdings holds a significant stake in Plethora Plastic Engineers — a forward-thinking plastic manufacturing company. This partnership strengthens our footprint in the manufacturing sector, enabling industrial development, innovation, and local economic empowerment.

Plethora specializes in custom plastic injection molding, product design and sustainable manufacturing solutions for various industries including automotive, packaging and consumer goods.

Through this strategic alliance, we leverage synergies to deliver comprehensive solutions to our clients while promoting sustainable manufacturing practices and local content development.
